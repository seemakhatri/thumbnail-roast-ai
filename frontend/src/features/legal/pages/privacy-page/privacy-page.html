At ThumbnailRoast, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture (if you sign in with Google).
  • Thumbnail images: The images you upload for analysis are stored temporarily to provide our service.
  • YouTube data: If you connect your YouTube account, we access video metadata and analytics to provide CTR correlation insights.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data: We collect anonymized data about how you interact with our service to improve the user experience.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and device type for analytics and optimization.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our thumbnail analysis service
  • To personalize your experience and show relevant recommendations
  • To communicate with you about updates, features, and promotions
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our AI models
  • To ensure compliance with our Terms of Service

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, with encryption at rest and in transit. Thumbnail images are stored for 30 days and then automatically deleted. We never share your personal information with third parties.

Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your account at any time
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Contact Us
